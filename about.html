About the Developer

My name is Peter Fox, and I am currently enrolled in my first quarter at Olympic College. I have been playing Cribbage for about five years, and enjoy playing with anyone, from pros to new players. Cribbage is easy to learn and takes about a half hour or less to play. This makes it a great game to play:

  • Between classes
  • Over lunch
  • On a ferry
  • With your family or friends

If you would like to get in touch with me to play a game of Crib, my email is peterfox@student.olympic.edu
